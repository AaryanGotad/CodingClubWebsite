ODP or the Online Degree Program Clubs at IIT Guwahati, is a student-driven initiative officially commenced on the 5th of March, 2025, in response to the enthusiastic interest shown by the students of the Online BSc. (Hons.) in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Program.

Recognizing the need for structured platforms that foster extracurricular and co-curricular engagement, IITG officially approved different clubs to enrich the learning experience beyond academics for the students of this degree program.

These clubs aim to create avenues for teamwork, creativity and networking among online degree students, encouraging meaningful connections, knowledge sharing and a dynamic learning environment.
